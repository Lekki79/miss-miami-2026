Official Miss Miami 2026 Rules

Eligibility Requirements

▼
  • Must be 18 years old or older as of November 15, 2026
  • Must be a citizen or permanent resident of the United States with ties to Miami or Florida
  • Must have a high school diploma or equivalent education
  • Prior pageant experience is not required
  • Open to all women regardless of marital status or family status

Application Requirements

▼
  • Complete all sections of the application form with accurate information
  • Submit a minimum of 3 professional-quality photos: headshot, full-length, and casual
  • Submit a minimum of 1 video introduction (60 seconds maximum) speaking about yourself and your goals
  • Pay the non-refundable $250 application fee
  • Accept all pageant rules and regulations by signing the agreement
  • Consent to the use of photos and videos for promotional purposes

Pre-Pageant Conduct

▼
  • Maintain professional behavior in all interactions with pageant staff and other contestants
  • Follow all social media guidelines set by Miss Miami
  • Attend all mandatory rehearsals and events
  • Respect all venue policies and regulations
  • Comply with health and safety protocols

Competition Criteria

▼
  • Interview (30%) - Communication skills, personality, and vision
  • Evening Gown (30%) - Elegance, confidence, and stage presence
  • Swimwear (20%) - Fitness, confidence, and poise
  • Overall Presence (20%) - Charisma, intelligence, and connection with audience

Judges' Decisions

▼
  • Judges' decisions are final and binding
  • No appeals process for scoring or placement
  • In case of a tie, judges will conduct a brief re-evaluation
  • Winners are determined by the judges' cumulative scores

Grounds for Disqualification

▼

Contestants may be disqualified for:

  • Providing false or misleading information on the application
  • Violating pageant conduct rules or venue policies
  • Engaging in disruptive or disrespectful behavior
  • Failure to attend mandatory events or rehearsals
  • Posting inappropriate content on social media that damages pageant reputation
  • Violation of any local, state, or federal laws

Rights & Permissions

▼
  • Miss Miami retains the right to use all contestant photos and videos for promotional purposes
  • Contestants grant Miss Miami permission to record and broadcast the pageant
  • Photos and videos may be shared on official social media accounts and website
  • Contestants will not receive compensation for use of their images
  • Miss Miami may feature contestants in press releases and media coverage

Refund Policy & Post-Pageant Responsibilities

▼

Refund Policy:

The $250 application fee is non-refundable after submission. Contestants who withdraw after being selected as finalists forfeit their entry fee. No refunds will be issued for any reason after payment is processed.

Post-Pageant Responsibilities (Winners):

  • The winner agrees to fulfill the duties of Miss Miami 2026 for one year
  • Must make public appearances and represent Miss Miami with grace and dignity
  • Must participate in community events, charity work, and promotional activities as requested
  • Must maintain professional conduct on and off social media
  • Must comply with all Miss Miami policies and procedures

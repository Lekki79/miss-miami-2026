Miss Miami 2026

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about applying, competing and joining the Miss Miami family.

Who can apply to Miss Miami?

+
Miss Miami is open to women between the ages of 18 and 28 who reside in or have a meaningful connection to South Florida. We welcome women of all backgrounds, ethnicities, and walks of life. Beauty is diverse — and so is Miss Miami.

Do I need prior pageant experience?

+
Not at all. Many of our past contestants and winners had never competed before. What matters most is your authenticity, your story, and your desire to grow. First-timers are absolutely encouraged to apply.

Is there a height or appearance requirement?

+
There are no height or specific appearance requirements. Miss Miami celebrates confidence, intelligence, and purpose alongside beauty. We believe every woman has something remarkable to offer.

Can international residents apply?

+
Applicants must be legally permitted to reside and work in the United States at the time of the competition. International residents with valid visas are welcome to apply, provided they meet all other eligibility requirements.

How do I apply?

+
Simply click Apply Now and complete the online application form. You'll need to fill in your personal information, answer a few essay questions, upload your photos, and pay the application fee. The entire process takes about 20–30 minutes.

What photos do I need to submit?

+
You'll need to submit three photos: a professional headshot, a full-length photo in formal or evening attire, and a casual photo that shows your personality. Photos must be recent (within 6 months), high resolution, and minimally edited. No heavy filters please.

When is the application deadline?

+
Applications for Miss Miami 2026 close on September 30, 2026. We strongly encourage applying early — spots are limited and we review applications on a rolling basis.

How long does the review process take?

+
After submitting, you'll receive a confirmation email. Our team will review your application and send the decision to the email address you provided.

What is the Contestant Portal?

+
The Contestant Portal is a private space for approved Miss Miami contestants. If your application is approved, you will receive a secure email invitation to activate access using the same email address provided in your application. Applications that are still under review do not have Portal access.

Can I edit my application after submitting?

+
Once submitted, applications cannot be edited through the portal. If you need to make a correction or update your information, please contact us directly within 48 hours of your submission.

What does the competition consist of?

+
The Miss Miami 2026 competition includes several stages: an interview round, talent showcase, evening gown, casual wear, and an on-stage Q&A. Contestants are scored on poise, articulation, presentation, and the strength of their personal platform.

When and where is the competition held?

+
Miss Miami 2026 takes place on November 15, 2026 in Miami, Florida. The venue will be announced to accepted contestants and ticket holders in September. It will be a fully produced stage event with live audience.

What is "Road to the Crown"?

+
Road to the Crown is our exclusive 8-step preparation program available to all accepted contestants through their personal online account. It guides you through everything from your platform development and interview prep to styling, stage presence, and final competition readiness. Think of it as your personal coach, available 24/7.

What do winners receive?

+
Miss Miami 2026 and her court receive a crown and sash, a prize package including cash award and sponsor gifts, a professional photoshoot, media features, and a year of official duties representing Miss Miami at events across South Florida.

Can family and friends attend the competition?

+
Yes! Each contestant receives 2 complimentary tickets for family or guests. Additional tickets will be available for purchase closer to the event date. Details will be shared with accepted contestants.

How much is the application fee?

+
The application and registration fee is $250 USD. This covers the cost of your application review, access to the Road to the Crown portal, one coaching session, and your contestant event package including 2 guest tickets.

What payment methods are accepted?

+
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), as well as Apple Pay and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Is the fee refundable?

+
The application fee is non-refundable. If your application is not selected to move forward, the fee covers the cost of review and administration. We appreciate your understanding and encourage you to apply again in future seasons.

Is my payment information secure?

+
Absolutely. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, one of the world's most trusted payment platforms. We never store your card details. All transactions are encrypted and PCI-compliant.

How can my brand become a sponsor?

+
Visit our Sponsors page to view partnership packages and submit an inquiry. We offer three tiers — Silver, Gold, and Crown — with benefits ranging from logo placement to full title sponsorship. Our team will respond within 48 hours.

What kind of brands do you partner with?

+
We partner with brands that align with our values of elegance, empowerment and purpose. Past and prospective partners include brands in beauty, fashion, wellness, hospitality, real estate, luxury goods and professional services. If you're unsure whether your brand is a fit, reach out — we love creative partnerships.

Can I create a custom sponsorship package?

+
Yes. In addition to our standard tiers, we offer fully custom partnership arrangements for brands with specific goals or budgets. Select "Custom / Other" in the sponsorship inquiry form and describe what you have in mind — we'll build a proposal around your needs.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

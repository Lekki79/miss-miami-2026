Who We Are

Redefining What a Crown Means

Miss Miami was founded on the belief that beauty pageants can be more than surface-level competition. We created a space where accomplished, driven women come together to celebrate what makes them extraordinary.

Our contestants are students, entrepreneurs, artists, athletes and advocates. They carry causes close to their hearts and the ambition to make real change in their communities.

Every year, Miss Miami brings together the brightest women of South Florida for an experience that challenges, transforms and elevates — whether you win the title or not, you leave as part of a lifelong sisterhood.
